Welcome

This is the collaboration site for support of the Topeka, KS Christmas Bureau. We're glad you're taking a look and wouldn't mind a hand if you've got some skills.

History

A group of volunteers from se2 joined with Let's Help and the United Way of Greater Topeka to develop better tools for the annual Christmas Bureau campaign. The Christmas Bureau's goal is to make sure that no one in our community goes with out a Christmas by providing a clearing house to "adopt" families for a Christmas. This process matches willing givers with families in need of a little (or lot) of help to provide presents, food, etc... to make a Merry Christmas for thousands of folks in the Topeka area.

Helping Us

Each year, volunteers develop, enhance and host systems used to collect data that matches "adoptees" with "adoptors". We use cloud hosted SaaS technology to bring this system to life each fall in support of the volunteers that run this amazing program. We can always use skilled developers, analysts, project managers, etc... to make the systems better each year.

This year we're making a significant upgrade to the system. We are moving from a ASP.NET based system to one based on the MEAN stack. This should make our software more usable, faster to develop and perform better. While it's all JavaScript, the language and frameworks aren't hard to learn.
